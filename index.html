About

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Levani, a passionate front-end web developer. I'm a first-year IT student at the University of Business and Technology. My skills include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React and Styled Components. I've always wanted to create something useful, meaningful, and visually appealing, which is why I started learning front-end development two years ago. I began my programming journey with a simple game called "Karel the Dog," and it immediately captured my interest. I would spend 7-8 hours a day playing it. I find immense satisfaction in implementing solutions for various problems, identifying the source of bugs, and resolving them. At different points in my journey, I faced challenges, including the demands of school, technical issues, or moments of boredom. However, I always returned to continue learning. Today, I've developed a strong coding habit. I make it a point to practice coding every day, regardless of any obstacles. This commitment is what continually drives my improvement as a developer. Apart from coding, my hobbies include playing video games, listening to and playing music, and staying active with fitness. These activities provide a balanced and enjoyable life outside of coding.
